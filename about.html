About Us

Who We Are!

We, at Fence Division, are committed to Reliable Fencing Solutions and impeccable customer service. We know our products will stand up against yellowing, surface peeling, rotting, splitting, rusting and flaking. By viewing our gallery, you can see the type of fences we have installed in the past.

Fence Division as a company is growing at an impeccable scale of over 360% every year. We have dedicated employees who are trained to deliver nothing but Best Services.

We deliver Property Fencing, High Security Fencing, Sports Fencing, Architectural Fencing and many other for Industrial, Residential and Commercial areas.

Fence Division’s clients include Airports, Hospitals, Golf Clubs, Massive Builders. Few notable clients include Bangalore Golf Club, Kannur International Airport, Sobha Emerald, Bangalore Baptist Hospital and so on…

With over 130 Projects under our hat, we are determined, to find your requirements a Reliable Solution!!
